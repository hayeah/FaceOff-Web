FaceOff

FaceOff is a Chrome Extension to implements the 2-click "like" button as seen on the German tech-news site Heise.

The reason I want a 2-click "like" button instead of blocking Facebook plugins outright is respect for content owner. If the content owner has a "like" button, then I should be willing to share the content if I find it worth sharing.

Mark Zuckerberg Is Watching You!

Did you know that...

Facebook is tracking you.

Every page with a "like" button is tracked by Facebook.

Even if you don't click on "like", you are still being tracked.

Facebook is going to make $1 Billion in 2011. They are not tracking you for fun.

 

Control Your Privacy!

This extension blocks Facebook from tracking you by replacing the "like" button with an image.

Because the real "like" button is not loaded, Facebook can't track you.

If you want to share something with your friend, click on the image. Only then would it transform into the real button.
